Aside from my tech side, I am quite heavily into my sports. My main sport is Field Hockey. I've been playing hockey for just about 15 years, I think.
I have spent a majority of my playing time at Leeds Hockey club. Making my way from their youth teams to the 2nd team. Ontop of the

I can quite happily say that Teesside University's team helped me develop myself as a person. I even ended up becoming chairman of the club.

I always say that i've come from a sporty family, this is down to my mum who has always been an advocate for being active.
Unlike my mum though I do have a more competative streak. I won't lie I do get quite competative about most things,

I should finally talk about what I have done in terms of filming and editing. I should start with my childhood goal of wanting to be an actor.
I took my dream of being an actor and joined Escape contemporary youth theatre, from here I performed in a variation of shows at various venues,
including Leeds City hall. From here the youth theatre decided to participate with the Golden Owl awards. The golden owls being a film award show,
specially set up for young filmmakers. We spent a good 4 weeks creating a piece called "What I would change" this is a piece which looks into young
peoples perspective of the world and what changes they would make to improve the lives of themselves and others no matter how big or small
the issues. During this process I got involved in both the acting side and the production side of the production. This became the catalyst
for my interest in film, TV and general media production. From there on it's been a lot of self-taught skills espically with my editing,
camera work, lighting and production documentations. I have only been able to improve and polish these skills throughout my time at university.
